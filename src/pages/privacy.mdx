# Privacy Policy

We care about privacy and would like to inform you about the information that is collected from you while you visit our website.

## Information We Collect

We don't directly collect information from you.

However, third-party services we use may collect data about your browsing behavior for targeted advertising purposes. Some of these [third parties](https://adsense.google.com/) are known for doing sketchy stuff, so check out their privacy policy if you're curious.

Our [hosting provider](https://vercel.com/) also collects some info about website traffic for performance and security purposes.

## Cookies

We don't track you, but we rely on third-party services that might. They may use cookies or tracking methods to collect information about your browsing habits and show you targeted advertisements.

## Data Protection

We make sure to protect your info with all the safety measures we can. But, since nothing is perfect, we can't promise that your data will be totally safe with us.

## Control of Your Information

Don't use our site if you don't want your information to be collected.

## Changes to Our Privacy Policy

We might make changes to our Privacy Policy every now and then. If you keep using the site, you agree to the new policy.
